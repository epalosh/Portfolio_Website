Formula SAE

USC Racing is an engineering design team that designs, builds, and tests a formula-style race car for international competition. We spend the entire year perfecting our design, and compete in May against other university teams.


As a member of the Electronics Subteam, my responsibilites include:

  • Implementation of a new ECU system.
  • Development of a custom dashboard to display various data points (wheel speed, oil temperature, etc.) for the driver.
  • Building, testing, and documenting the wiring harness in RapidHarness CAD software.
  • Recording data using CAN network.
  • Problem-solving in a demanding environment.


Formula SAE is one of the most involved and hands-on extracurricular choices for undergraduate engineering students.
